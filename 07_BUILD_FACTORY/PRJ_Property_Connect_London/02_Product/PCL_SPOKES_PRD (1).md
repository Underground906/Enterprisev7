# PCL SPOKES PRD
## Magazine, Newsletter, Podcast, Video/CTV, Social

---

## OVERVIEW

The Spokes are PCL's content publishing ecosystem — five interconnected media channels that feed each other and drive audience to the platform.

```
NEWSLETTER (Spearhead - Weekly)
    ↓ feeds
MAGAZINE (Monthly Print + Digital)
    ↓ feeds
PODCAST (Weekly Audio)
    ↓ feeds
VIDEO/CTV (Ongoing Library + Channel)
    ↓ all feed
SOCIAL (Daily Distribution)
    ↓ all drive to
PLATFORM (Signup + Engagement)
```

---

## PROPRIETARY SYSTEMS

### 1. The Rotational Reach Maximizer™ (Magazine)
- 12 boroughs per year, one per month
- 50,000 readers per borough = 600,000 annual reach
- Prestige print + digital amplification

### 2. The Expert Content Catalyst™ (All Spokes)
- Audience-first content framework
- 5 stages: Audience → Value → Story → Amplify → Convert
- Turns professional expertise into trust-building content

### 3. The Content Multiplier Engine™ (Video/CTV)
- 1 filming day = 10-20 videos
- Multi-format capture (tours, interviews, how-tos)
- Feeds CTV, YouTube, social, newsletter, magazine QR codes

### 4. The Authority Amplifier™ (Podcast)
- Expert interviews that build thought leadership
- Multi-channel repurposing (audio → video → article → social)
- Evergreen content library

## CROSS-SPOKE INTEGRATION

### Content Flow

```
1. Research generates idea
   ↓
2. Newsletter publishes first (daily)
   ↓
3. Popular content becomes magazine feature (monthly)
   ↓
4. Feature expert becomes podcast guest (weekly)
   ↓
5. Podcast becomes video content (ongoing)
   ↓
6. Video clips feed social (daily)
   ↓
7. Social drives newsletter signups (loop)
```

### Cross-Promotion

| From | To | Method |
|------|-----|--------|
| Newsletter | Magazine | "Read more in this month's issue" |
| Newsletter | Podcast | "Listen to the full interview" |
