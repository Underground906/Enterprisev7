## MARKET CONTEXT

### The Property Investment Landscape in London

**Market Size:**
- 2.8M private rented homes in London
- 400,000+ individual landlords
- 15,000+ professional investors (5+ properties)
- £2.1T total residential property value
- £45B+ annual transaction volume

**Investor Types:**
- Single property landlords (accidental or intentional)
- Portfolio landlords (2-10 properties)
- Professional investors (10-50 properties)
- Institutional investors (50+ units/BTR)
- Development investors (land to profit)
- International investors (safe haven)

**Why Investors Need AI:**
- Deal analysis is time-intensive
- Good deals go fast (speed matters)
- Market intelligence is scattered
- Tax optimization is complex
- Scale is hard without systems
- Competition for deals is fierce

**The Opportunity:**
Investors are data-driven decision makers. They'll pay premium for tools that find deals, analyze faster, and manage portfolios efficiently. They understand ROI.
