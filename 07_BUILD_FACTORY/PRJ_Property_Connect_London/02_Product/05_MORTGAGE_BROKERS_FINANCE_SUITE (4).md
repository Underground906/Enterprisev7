## MARKET CONTEXT

### The Mortgage Broker Landscape

**Market Size (UK):**
- 80%+ of mortgages arranged through brokers
- £300B+ annual mortgage lending
- 12,000+ mortgage broker firms
- 50,000+ individual mortgage advisors
- Average commission: £500-2,000 per case

**London Specifics:**
- Higher property values = higher procuration fees
- Complex cases more common (income types, visa status)
- BTL market concentrated in London
- International buyer specialization opportunities

**Why Brokers Need AI:**
- Lead qualification is time-consuming
- Same questions asked repeatedly
- Document chasing is painful
- Application tracking is manual
- Market changes constantly (rate updates)
- Compliance burden increasing

**The Opportunity:**
Mortgage brokers are relationship-driven. AI handles the admin, they handle the advice. More conversations, less paperwork = more cases.

**3. Eligibility Assessment**
- Age check

**4. Scenario Calculation**

- Complexity assessment (standard, complex, specialist)
- Likelihood to proceed

**6. Warm Handover**

- Captures full contact details
- Sends summary to advisor

**7. Document Preparation**
- Sends checklist based on case type

**Why This Has Long-Term Value:**
