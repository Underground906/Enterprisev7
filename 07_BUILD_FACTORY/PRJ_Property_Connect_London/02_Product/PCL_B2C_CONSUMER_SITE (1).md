# PCL B2C CONSUMER SITE

**Purpose:** Promote PCL's media ecosystem and platform to consumers (buyers, renters, sellers, investors, etc.)

**Style:** Clean, modern brochure site. Static pages. Drives to signup, subscribe, or contact.
