# Copywriting Formulas

## Headlines That Convert

A collection of copywriting formulas for writing effective headlines.

### AIDA Formula
- Attention: Grab their attention
- Interest: Build interest with benefits
- Desire: Create desire for the solution
- Action: Call to action

### PAS Formula
- Problem: Identify the problem
- Agitation: Agitate the pain
- Solution: Present the solution
