## MARKET CONTEXT

### The Lettings Landscape in London

**Market Size:**
- 2.8M private rented households in London
- 3,500+ letting agencies
- Average rent: £2,100/month (£25,200/year)
- Letting fee: 8-12% of annual rent (£2,000-3,000)
- Management fee: 10-15% monthly
- Renewals, check-ins, check-outs: Additional fees

**The Regulatory Tsunami:**
- Renters Reform Bill
- Selective Licensing (expanding)
- EPC minimum C by 2028
- Awaab's Law (response times)
- Deposit protection rules
- Right to Rent requirements
- Gas/Electrical safety mandates
- HMO licensing complexity

**Why This Sector is PERFECT for AI:**
- High volume, repetitive tasks
- Compliance is complex and risky
- Tenant communication is constant
- Maintenance coordination is chaos
- Landlords demand transparency
- Staff turnover is high (training cost)
- Margins are being squeezed

**The Opportunity:**
Letting agents who don't adopt AI will drown in compliance and lose landlords to those who do. You're selling survival + competitive advantage.
