# Property Market Research Note

## London Estate Agents Analysis

Looking at the property market in London, particularly for estate agents and letting agents.

Key points:
- Property prices continue to rise in prime London areas
- Estate agents are adopting AI chatbots for customer service
- Letting agents need better lead qualification systems
- The buy-to-let market is showing resilience
